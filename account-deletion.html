📱 How to Delete in App

Follow these simple steps to remove your account inside the app

01

Open FarmGro App

Launch the FarmGro app on your mobile device and ensure you're logged in to your account.

02

Navigate to Profile

Tap on your profile icon located at the bottom navigation bar to open your profile screen.

03

Open Personal Information

In the Account Settings section, tap on 'Personal Info' to access your personal information screen.

04

Go to Account Actions

Scroll down to find the 'Account Actions' section and tap on 'Delete Account' (displayed in red).

05

Review Warning & Continue

A warning dialog will appear showing what will be deleted. Review the information and tap 'Continue' to proceed.

06

Confirm by Typing DELETE

Type 'DELETE' in the confirmation box, optionally provide a reason, and tap 'Delete My Account'. Your request will be reviewed within 24-27 hours.
