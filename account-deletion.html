Permanent Action

Delete Your
FarmGro Account

Follow the steps below to permanently delete your FarmGro account. This action cannot be undone.

⚠️ Important Notice

Please read carefully before proceeding

  • Deleting your account is a permanent action and cannot be reversed. Once deleted, the following will occur:
  • Your personal data (full name, mobile number, email address, delivery addresses) will be removed from our active databases.
  • Order history and transactional data may be retained for a limited period solely to comply with legal and tax obligations as per our Privacy Policy.
  • You will lose access to your account, order history, and saved preferences within the App.
  • Any pending orders or transactions must be completed before account deletion.
  • This action is irreversible — once deleted, you will need to create a new account to use FarmGro again.

🔑 Information Required

To verify your identity

Registered Phone Number
The number used to create your account.
Account Access
You must be logged into your account.

📱 STEP-BY-STEP GUIDE

How to Delete Your Account

01

Open FarmGro App

Launch the FarmGro app on your mobile device and ensure you're logged in to your account.

02

Navigate to Profile

Tap on your profile icon located at the bottom navigation bar to open your profile screen.

03

Open Personal Information

In the Account Settings section, tap on 'Personal Info' to access your personal information screen.

04

Go to Account Actions

Scroll down to find the 'Account Actions' section and tap on 'Delete Account' (displayed in red).

05

Review Warning & Continue

A warning dialog will appear showing what will be deleted. Review the information and tap 'Continue' to proceed.

06

Confirm by Typing DELETE

Type 'DELETE' in the confirmation box, optionally provide a reason, and tap 'Delete My Account'. Your request will be reviewed within 24-27 hours.
